
**What is Unbounded?**
Unbounded is an animation first combat game prototype built around expressive motion and system-driven storytelling. The demo focuses on stylized martial arts combat inspired by Muay Boran, blending hand crafted animation with experimental gameplay mechanics that react to player behavior.  Unbounded explores how animation, timing, and game systems can communicate narrative indirectly, without relying on exposition or traditional cutscenes. This project represents my interest in animation, game development, and engineering through interactive design.

**How was the animations made and the characters too?**
The game was create using Procreate and Rough Animator. The sprites and start screen were all hand drawn, with inspration from Cuphead and Hollow Knight. As of now, the story for the game is fleshed out but has yet to be animated. All boss fights have been created on paper but are yet to be made in game due to this being a demo as of now. The characters were created through a concept sketch, then implemented in game with rough drawings.

**What can you actually play in the demo**
As of now, the only two scenes that you can run is the start screen and the Ghost Of Khan boss fight. However the Ghost of Khan boss fight doesnt currently have animations for attacks XD. In the demo, it will eventually have an arrow attack, and melee sword attack.

**What are the Controls?**
Q to Dodge Left
E to Dodge Right
W to Slip Back; Move your head back
S to Duck
A to Move Left
D to Move Right
Shift to Dash
Space to Jump
R to Restart
Space and Enter to skip (Only At Start Screen)
